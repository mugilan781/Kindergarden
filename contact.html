FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our school, enrollment, programs, and more.

We serve children from ages 2 through 11, spanning our Nursery program (2–3 years), Pre-K (3–4 years), Kindergarten (4–5 years), and Primary School (Grades 1–5). Each program is thoughtfully designed to meet the developmental needs of its age group.

Enrollment begins with scheduling a campus tour and meeting with our admissions team. After the tour, you'll complete an application form, submit required documents (birth certificate, immunization records, etc.), and pay a registration fee. We accept enrollments year-round, space permitting. You can start the process by filling out our contact form or calling us directly.

We offer three internationally recognized programs: Montessori (ages 2–6), Cambridge International (ages 5–11), and the IB Primary Years Programme (ages 3–12). Our approach blends the best of these frameworks with play-based learning, inquiry-driven exploration, and a strong emphasis on social-emotional development.

We maintain low student-to-teacher ratios to ensure every child receives individualized attention: Nursery & Pre-K (1:6), Kindergarten (1:8), and Primary Grades 1–5 (1:10). Our classrooms also include teaching assistants and specialist teachers for enrichment programs.

Yes! Our extended care program runs from 7:00 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday through Friday. It includes breakfast care, after-school homework support, enrichment activities, and free play — all supervised by our trained staff. Extended care is available at an additional cost and can be added to any enrollment plan.

Safety is our top priority. Our campus features secure access with video intercoms, CCTV monitoring in common areas, a nut-free campus policy, trained first-aid staff, regular safety drills, and a secure pickup/drop-off system with ID verification. All staff undergo background checks and annual safety training.
