03Fees & Payments

Tuition and fees are set annually and communicated to enrolled families before the start of each academic year. The following payment policies apply:

  • Tuition is due on or before the first day of each month, semester, or as outlined in your specific enrollment agreement. Late payments may incur a fee of 5% of the outstanding amount per month.
  • An enrollment deposit equal to one month's tuition is required to secure your child's place. This deposit is applied to the final month's tuition and is non-refundable if enrollment is cancelled after the start of the academic term.
  • Kindergarden accepts payments via bank transfer, credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), and certified check. Personal checks are accepted up to 30 days before the end of the academic year.
  • Full or partial tuition assistance is available for qualifying families. Assistance applications are reviewed confidentially by our financial aid committee. Awards are applied as a credit to your account and do not constitute a scholarship or merit-based reduction.
  • Refunds for early withdrawal are calculated on a pro-rata basis for the remaining term, less a processing fee of $250. No refunds are issued after 50% of the academic term has elapsed.
  • All fees are quoted in United States Dollars (USD) and are exclusive of applicable taxes unless otherwise stated. Kindergarden is not responsible for any bank charges, currency conversion fees, or intermediary bank deductions.

04Code of Conduct

Kindergarden is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all students, families, and staff. The following behaviors are expected from every member of our community:

  • Students are expected to treat classmates, teachers, and staff with kindness, respect, and empathy at all times. Bullying, harassment, intimidation, or exclusionary behavior is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action.
  • Parents and guardians are expected to communicate with staff in a courteous and constructive manner. Verbal abuse, threats, or aggressive behavior toward any Kindergarden employee will not be tolerated and may result in termination of enrollment.
  • The use of electronic devices, including smartphones, tablets, and smartwatches, is restricted during school hours unless explicitly authorized by a teacher for educational purposes. Kindergarden is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged personal devices.
  • Violence, possession of weapons (including toy weapons that resemble real firearms), theft, vandalism, or any illegal activity on school grounds will result in immediate suspension pending review, and may lead to permanent expulsion.
  • Kindergarden promotes a drug-, alcohol-, and tobacco-free environment. Any student found to be in possession of or under the influence of prohibited substances will face disciplinary action in accordance with school policy and applicable law.
  • Repeated violations of the code of conduct may result in a behavior contract, probation, suspension, or termination of enrollment at the sole discretion of the school administration.
