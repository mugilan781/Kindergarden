Welcome to Kindergarden

A World of Wonder
Awaiting Your Child

Founded in 1999, Kindergarden International has been a beacon of educational excellence, blending the best of Montessori, Cambridge, and IB PYP methodologies into a harmonious learning journey for children aged 2 to 12.

Our sun-drenched campus features dedicated art studios, a children's kitchen, a music room, expansive outdoor gardens, and a library stocked with over 8,000 books. Every corner is designed to spark curiosity.

Our Programs

Designed for Every
Stage of Discovery

From toddler exploration to primary school mastery, our four signature programs create a seamless educational journey rooted in inquiry, creativity, and academic rigour.

Montessori

Ages 2–6. Self-directed, hands-on learning with specially designed materials in a prepared environment.

Practical Life Sensorial Language

Cambridge

Ages 6–12. Internationally recognized curriculum with a strong foundation in English, Maths, and Science.

English Mathematics Science

IB PYP

Inquiry Action Reflection

Why Choose Us

What Makes Kindergarden
Truly Special

Every detail of our school is designed with intention — from our research-backed curriculum to our nurturing community. Here's what sets us apart.

Inquiry-Based Learning

Children drive their own learning through questions and exploration, developing critical thinking skills that last a lifetime.

Small Class Sizes

With a maximum of 12 students per class, every child receives the personalized attention they deserve to thrive.

Safe & Nurturing

Secure campus with CCTV, keycard access, trained first-aid staff, and a wellness program that prioritizes emotional safety.

Bilingual Immersion

Full English and Mandarin immersion program with native-speaking teachers in both languages from age 2.

Nature Campus

Our 2-acre campus features edible gardens, a butterfly habitat, outdoor classrooms, and natural playgrounds.

Global Community

Students from 15+ nationalities create a rich multicultural tapestry where diversity is celebrated every day.
